Death of the innocents

"When Herod realized that he had been outwitted by the Magi, he was furious, and he gave orders to kill all the boys in Bethlehem and its vicinity who were two years old and under" (Matthew 2:16)

In order to try to eradicate the threat to his kingdom by this newborn king, Herod takes extreme measures. This unpleasant turn of events already shows the opposition to Jesus that is brewing and reveals that evil that His life will provoke.
