Remix A Christmas Carol

  • Creative challenge:
  • Find a Christmas song that you love
  • Change the words or add a new verse or bridge to it
  • Record your song using audio or video as a solo, in a group singing, in a band, or digitally produce it.
  • Upload the video or audio to soundcloud/youtube
  • Share it on Twitter with Hashtags #AdventHunt15 #Submit
  • Send the song to a loved one
  • Listen to other people's remixes: RT or favourite others if you like them!
